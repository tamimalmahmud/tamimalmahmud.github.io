Short Bio

I am a PhD student in Computer Engineering at the University of Rovira i Virgili, Tarragona, Spain. My research focuses on Security and Privacy in Large Language Models (LLMs). The aim is to establish effective unlearning techniques for LLMs to comply with privacy regulations such as GDPR. It involves developing innovative methods for selective data elimination, improving model retraining procedures, and ensuring scalability and robustness.

Research Interests
